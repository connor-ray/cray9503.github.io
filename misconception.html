Biggest misconceptions in the coffee world!

  • Dark roast has more caffine.

    • False! The lighter the roast the more caffine and flavor. Essentially the more you roast a bean the more it is being cooked therefore, killing off flavor and caffine.

  • Coffee is made from beans.

    • Yes, we may call them beans, but they are actually seeds from a fruit similar to a cherry.

  • Espresso has more caffine than regular coffee.

    • Espresso may be the most dense form of coffee. However, there is about 95-200mg of caffine in every standard cup of coffee (8oz). As opposed to 45-74mg of caffine in a standard shot (1oz)
